PRAVASI BHARATIYA DIVAS 2023

Pravasi Bharatiya Divas is celebrated once in every two years to strengthen the engagement of the overseas Indian community with the Government of India and reconnect them with their roots. The 17th PBD Convention is being held Virtually on 9 January, 2023 in Indore. The theme of 17th PBD Convention 2023 is "Contributing to Aatmanirbhar Bharat". Read More
